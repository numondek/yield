About Yieldup Dev.

We are awakening POSSIBILITIES in DISABILITIES by closing the gaps in literacy and empowerment of youth with disabilities by constructing sustainable libraries in rural environments using reused plastic bottles, facilitating reading and creativity development in a supportive environment with counseling, technology and therapy animals.

See the various projects we have ongoing

BALE

Project BALE combats prevalent illiteracy through a center built by turning waste plastic bottles into sustainable eco-friendly inclusive libraries.

LEADvocacy

This combines leadership and advocacy in empowering individuals with practical skills in solving unmet needs in communities
TeachAbility

An initiative focused on training individuals on Inclusive education, eco-inclusion and DEIA
