About Yieldup Development Initiative

We are awakening POSSIBILITIES in DISABILITIES

We do this by closing the gaps in literacy and empowerment of youth with disabilities by constructing sustainable libraries in rural environments using reused plastic bottles, facilitating reading and creativity development in a supportive environment with counseling, technology and therapy animals.

our Vision

To have society that is inclusive for all

We envision a society that is inclusive for all, empowers Persons With Disabilities to lead initiatives and inspires positive change in communities.

our Mission
To bridging the gaps in discrimination and illiteracy

We are bridging the gaps in discrimination and illiteracy for persons with disabilities holistically through education, creativity, disability advocacy, technology and therapy.

How we help our community

Looking at our vision for an inclusive society. We are committed to seeing abilities in disabilities by empowering people with disabilities through literacy and creative development. Our core mission is to bridge the gaps in discrimination by constructing sustainable libraries in rural areas, using eco-friendly materials like reused plastic bottles.

These libraries provide not only access to books, but also a nurturing environment that encourages reading, creativity, personal development. We offer counseling, assistance, and therapy animals to foster a supportive and inclusive space for every individual improving their health and well-being.

Transforming rural areas and PWDs through innovation, leadership, and sustainability for a brighter, inclusive future.
